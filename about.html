About Me

Snapshot of My Life

I've lived most of my life in Sun Prairie, Wisconsin. Just north of our home were miles and miles of rolling countryside: dairy farms, cornfields, and forests. I truly grew to love the outdoors as I traveled around Wisconsin and visited the many beautiful regions.

My family is amazing! I have seven siblings, most of them younger than me. Our entire family loves the outdoors, especially kayaking in the many lakes and rivers around the state. We also enjoy traveling. Although we don't leave the country very often, we've seen most of the stunning geography of the United States.

I served a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Argentina and northern California (due to COVID-19). Since then, I've been studying at Brigham Young Univeristy in Provo, Utah. I love the college, its campus, and the magnificent places to explore here in Utah.
